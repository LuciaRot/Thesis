Mechanics

The mechanics are part of the skeleton of your game.

There are chore mechanics which are the most apparent and most used and most important mechanics in the game.

There are also secondary mechanics that add to the core mechanics but aren't as important. Which ones these are totally depends on the game you're trying to make.

If you want to make a jump and run game you're core mechanics would be jumping and running.

One of the most fundamental mechanics in your game are rules. They define the course of action. What can the player do? What is he supposed to do?
